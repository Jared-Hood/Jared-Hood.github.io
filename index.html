About Me

Thank you for visiting my webpage. My name is Jared Hood and I’m a fourth year student at the University of Virginia pursuing a B.S. in computer science. Programming has become my passion and as a result I have created innovative projects, which you can check out below. When I am not programming, I enjoy working with my hands and the intricate work of repairing bikes and, as a result, started my own business, Jared’s Bike Repairs. The business is based out of Charlottesville, VA and primarily supports students. While school, programming and bike repairs encompasses my current life, my future plan is to travel the world and, maybe, even space.
